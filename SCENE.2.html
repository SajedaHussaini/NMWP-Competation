Amina-jan, the old teacher, risked everything. Every night, she gathered them beneath the walnut tree — ten girls, ten candles of hope. She taught them what they tried to erase, showing them that the mind, once awakened, can never be shackled. Each lesson she gave them was a spark in the dark, a tiny light that could never be extinguished. Amina-jan risked everything for them — for their future, for their voices. And as long as she lives, she will carry her courage inside her.
