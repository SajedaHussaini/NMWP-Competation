Laila stands before the crowd

Laila stood before the crowd, her heart pounding steadily within her chest. Hundreds of faces gazed at her, yet she did not falter. Though her voice trembled, it did not break.

“Education is not a crime,” she declared, her words resonating clearly through the air. The reaction was immediate — threats of death and acts of violence followed, but Laila no longer felt fear. Her truth had been spoken, and it could not be erased. The voice that had once been silenced had now discovered its power.
