After finding refuge with the woman who ran a small school for runaway girls, she continued to struggle with the remnants of her past. Even in the safety of the shelter, nights were filled with reflection and determination. She would write secretly, often on scraps of paper, inscribing, “If I survive, I will teach the world the true meaning of freedom.” While survival had become less immediate of a concern, she knew that her personal journey toward empowerment and self-realization was far from complete.
